About Nathan

Hello!
My name is Nathan, I am 17 years old, and I am a Christian and I live in the United States. This website is where I put what I consider to be my best creations: a portfolio. Most activities on this website are ones that I have picked up within the last 4 years, since I was about 13. Before then, I did not even know what a video editor was, or what frames in a movie were. But that is not to say that those first 13 years of my life did not contribute to these activities. On the contrary, I believe that my education has greatly contributed to any successes that I may have.
I have been home schooled for all of my life, and my parents were very intentional about my education. I owe much to the ideas that they instilled in me. Without them it is doubtful whether I would have ever created any of the things seen on this website. Although I was not explicitly taught any of these things in school, my parents instilled in me a healthy curiosity about how things work, why they work, and how to make them work. Without this inquisitive mindset I would never be able to persist in my hobbies, all of which are time consuming and have delayed gratification, and no guaranteed success.
My parents have been very supportive of my hobbies, and very understanding of their requirements. They supported me even when I had little results. They are my biggest fans, and I really appreciate that. I love my family, they are the best.
About This Website
As previously mentioned this is my portfolio website. Why did I decide to create this site? The answer is not to toot my own horn. The purpose of this site is to accurately reflect the skills that I have, with no embellishments. What you see is what you get. I do not believe in social media in general, and I want a place to share my work with people. So if I tell someone that I am an animator and they ask to see some of my work, I can refer them here.
I also have dreams of doing one, some or all of these activities as a job one day. If ever I apply for a job in one of these fields I can send this website to my possible employer as a reflection of my abilities. I have a photo editing job, and that is a good start, but editing endless furniture photos is not my dream.
Being naturally introverted, I tend to never to share any of my works with other people. My own family often does not see my creations. I am not one to spontaneously talk about myself. My hope is that this website will contain all of the information that anyone ever needs to know about me, so that I hardly ever have to talk about myself again.
Who is Nathan?
As previously mentioned, my name is Nathan and I am 17 years old. What do I look like? Where do I live? How tall am I? What is my last name? What is my cousin's brother's aunt's dad's favourite food truck? These are all questions that I do not seek to answer. All you need to know about me is that my name is Nathan, I am 17, I am a Christian, and I am an American.
I naturally do not trust the internet, so why would I trust it with a picture of myself? I do not wish to have my image in a database. There are plenty of Americans, plenty of Nathans, and plenty of teenagers, so I figured that this discription of myself still gives me a decent amount of obscurity.
Why bother telling you I am a Christian? Because that is something that I do want you to know. I wish it to be my difining feature, the most obvious thing about me. Since there are objective standards of beauty and creativity set by God I am free to create what honors God, and therefor is pleasing to me. If you have any questions about Christianity I would highly encourage you to go to visit Answers In Genesis, where you can find many answers to any questions you may have. And most importantly, pick up a Bible. Although the Bible can be intimidating, it is the infallible truth. Begin in the book of Matthew in order to grasp the gospel message.
