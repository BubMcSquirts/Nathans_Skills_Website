Music
From ages 4 to 14 I played the piano as my instrument. I performed in recitals and took lessons for over 10 years. However, in 2020 I was forced to stop taking piano lessons because of Covid, and I have not taken lessons since, although I can still play. In 2020 I discovered electronic music production through the music instructor Taetro, and when I was 15 I got my first midi keyboard and a copy of the DAW(Digital Audio Workstation) FL Studio. I began making music with that and greatly enjoyed it. In 2021 I got a bigger keyboard and a copy of Ableton Live, which has become my primary Daw. I enjoy Ableton's workflow more, although FL Studio is still better for some things. Here are some of my electronically produced songs. The ones here were made in Ableton Live with an Artutia MiniLab MK II midi controller.
Then, in late 2021 I got a guitar. I taught myself to play it. Guitar has become my primary instrument, and I enjoy playing it very much. Here is a video of me performing Blackbird by The Beatles.
Blackbird by The Beatles: I performed this on the Taylor GS Mini using a Shure SM57 microphone recorded into Ableton Live. Blackbird is probably one of the most popular finger-style guitar songs.
I learned from free resources, and benefited from many people. As previously mentioned I learned electronic music production from Taetro, and I learned guitar from primarily Justin from Justin Guitar and Marty from Marty Music.
